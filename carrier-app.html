Step 6 of 6

One last step — authorize your driving record.

Agencies need your Motor Vehicle Record to give you a firm, bindable quote. We pull it once, securely, with your permission.

What is an MVR and why do we need it?
Your MVR is your official driving history from the state DMV — violations, accidents, license status, and endorsements. It's the single most important document agencies use to price commercial trucking insurance. By pulling it now, your profile is 100% complete and agencies can give you firm, bindable quotes without calling you for more information.
